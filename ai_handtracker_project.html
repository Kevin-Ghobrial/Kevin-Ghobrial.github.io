Description

AuralSense Hand Tracker: an AI-driven device audio control. To make this, I used computer vision and machine learning to track hand movements in real-time. Compatible with various audio systems, it connects seamlessly and requires minimal setup.

Operating the AuralSense Hand Tracker is intuitive: raise your finger to increase volume and lower it to decrease. Its advanced algorithms ensure precise adjustments with every gesture. Beyond volume control, it enables track switching, play/pause commands, and customizable gestures.

Adaptable to different lighting conditions, it maintains accuracy in any environment. Privacy is prioritized, with all data processed locally for security.
